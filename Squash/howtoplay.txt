:::::How to play::::::
Don't let the ball hit the floor more than once consecutively.

:::::Keyboard bindings:::::
Movement:
Up - W
Down - S
Left - A
Right - D

Swing racquet:
Up swing - Space
Forward swing - Ctrl-Space

Switch ball:
Squashball - 1
Football-sized ball with squashball's mass - 2
Football - 3

Other:
Toggle force vectors display - K
Toggle between sandbox and game mode - M
Toggle between player and ball view - B
Pause - P
